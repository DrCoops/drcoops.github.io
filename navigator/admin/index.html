Priority overrides (RAG)

Use these to adjust how the Navigator rates each option for your local context — regardless of what the scoring logic would normally produce. "Default" means the standard scoring applies. Setting an option to Green, Amber or Red will override the score for all users.

For example: if your organisation has no SAC, you might set SAC/Expert Group to Red. Or if your organisation has a particularly active College of Experts, you might promote it to Green.
